Why we built this

Food waste starts with not knowing what to cook.

🥕

Start from your fridge, not a shopping list

Most recipe apps assume you're about to go grocery shopping. MealMind starts from what's already sitting there.

🛡️

Allergy-safe by design

Recipes are filtered against your stated allergies before generation — not a promise made after the fact.

💬

Talk to it like a person

No dropdowns or filters to configure. Say what you have in plain language and get a real answer back.

Our mission

Nothing in your kitchen should go to waste for lack of a good idea.

MealMind AI was built for anyone who cooks occasionally, doesn't want to overthink it, and would rather use what's already there than order in. It's a class project, not a startup pitch — but the problem it's solving is a real one.
